Frequently Asked Questions
What types of antiques do you specialize in?
We offer a wide selection of antique furniture, artwork, jewelry, collectibles, and historical items from the 18th to 20th century.
Are your antiques certified or authenticated?
Yes. Each item is reviewed and authenticated by experts before being added to our collection.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer secure global shipping with full insurance and tracking on all orders.
Can I visit your physical store?
Absolutely! We are located in Canada. Please check our contact page for directions and hours.
How can I know the value of an antique I own?
We offer appraisal services. Contact us with images and details of your item for an expert evaluation.
Do you accept custom search requests for rare items?
Yes, let us know what you're looking for and we’ll try to locate it through our network of suppliers and collectors.
Can I return an item if I change my mind?
Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery if the item is in its original condition.
Are prices negotiable?
Some prices are flexible depending on the item. Feel free to contact us for offers or bulk discounts.
How do I track my order?
After dispatch, we’ll send you a tracking code via email or SMS to monitor your shipment.
What happens if my order arrives damaged?
We take care in packing, but if damage occurs, contact us within 48 hours for a full resolution or refund.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for select high-value items. Contact us to learn more.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. We use SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect all transactions.
Do you sell reproductions or only originals?
Our store only offers genuine and original antique items, not replicas or reproductions.
Do your items come with certificates of authenticity?
Yes. Valuable antiques come with documented proof of origin and authenticity when applicable.
Do you buy antiques from customers?
Yes. If you own a valuable antique, send us details and photos. We’ll evaluate and make an offer if it fits our collection.
Do you offer gift packaging?
Yes, we offer elegant gift packaging on request. You can select this option during checkout.
How often is your catalog updated?
We add new antique items every week. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
Can I book a virtual tour of your gallery?
Yes. We offer video call walkthroughs and consultations for serious buyers.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and some cryptocurrencies.
Can I reserve an item before purchasing?
Yes, items can be reserved for up to 48 hours on request.
